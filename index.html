RIT Construct Portal

Project Synopsis

The Construct is RIT's on campus makerspace which provides resources and machines to all students who are interested in 3D printing, woodwork, machining and electronics. Currently The Construct serves about 1200-1500 students per year and is located inside of a couple connected rooms spanning about 2,000 sq ft within Institute Hall.
The Construct will be moving inside The SHED, a newly constructed building on campus. There, it will be expanded to 20,000 sq ft, housing many different machines and dedicated areas throughout each floor, including a point-of-sale-based shop for materials. Due to the increase in size and resources the makerspace will be using, the current management software system will not be adequate moving forward.
Our team's goal is to build upon the work of a previous team to develop a portal to manage to occupants and users of this space, providing a means by which students can access the lab, pay for materials, and train for the use of this machinery. This project is open sourced and is meant to offer other colleges a roadmap for developing makerspace management portals of their own.
